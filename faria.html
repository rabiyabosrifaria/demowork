Story of Rajshahi station

Rajshahi Railway Station is a railway station at Rajshahi, Bangladesh.[1] The railway station is the main station of the city, and links to Dhaka via the Iswardi–Sirajganj line.[2] The station is one of the most modern, largest and important stations of Bangladesh Railway along with Dhaka and Chittagong railway station. Headquarter of Western Zone of Bangladesh Railway is also located within the station complex.[3]
